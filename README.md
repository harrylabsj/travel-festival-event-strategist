# Festival and Event Travel Strategist
Strategic planning for festival and event-based travel experiences — from timing optimization to crowd management.
## What it does
- Provides a structured **event research framework** to evaluate festivals for authenticity, cost, and ROI before committing
- Delivers **timing and logistics optimization** including booking windows, routing, and accommodation strategies
- Offers **crowd management planning** with anti-crush strategies, alternative venues, and peak-avoidance scheduling
## Example scenarios
**1. 五一/国庆挤人堆攻略**
> 👤 只有法定假日能出去玩，但每次五一国庆出门就是看人头。有没有既能在热门时间出门又不那么痛苦的方法？
> 🤖 提供反向时间轴策略（假期第1天宅家+最后1天去景点人流少70%）、目的地选择技巧（热门城市非热门区域）、本地人攻略（搜周边菜市场/社区公园）、住宿技巧（地铁终点站附近）等全套法定假日逃人方案。

**2. 春节返乡顺带看灯会**
> 👤 过年回老家洛阳，想顺便带爸妈去看灯会，又怕老人孩子受不了排队。怎么安排最省心？
> 🤖 输出春节灯会家庭出行方案——提前3天预约夜场票避开高峰期、17:30前入场占便利位置、为老人走无障碍通道、给孩子准备暖宝宝零食包、地铁沿线住宿避免散场堵车、雨天备选室内博物馆方案。

**3. Plan a trip around Holi in India**
> 👤 Plan a trip around Holi in India, Diwali, and a local village harvest festival — all in one trip.
> 🤖 Festival-calendar-aligned itinerary with date-flexible routing, each festival's logistics (best city, crowd strategy, cultural etiquette, safety, photography rules), accommodation booking window, and local-fixer recommendations.
